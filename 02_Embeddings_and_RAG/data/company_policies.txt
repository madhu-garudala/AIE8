Company Policies and Procedures

Remote Work Policy: Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
Vacation Policy: All employees receive 20 days of paid vacation annually.
Dress Code: Business casual attire is required in the office.
Office Hours: Standard office hours are 9 AM to 5 PM, Monday through Friday.
Meeting Policy: All meetings should have a clear agenda and be limited to 60 minutes.
